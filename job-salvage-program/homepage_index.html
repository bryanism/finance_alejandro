Finance and Accounting Calculator

Acid-Test Ratio:
"The acid-test ratio uses a firm's balance sheet
data as an indicator of whether it has sufficient short-term
assets to cover its short-term liabilities."

Debt Ratio:
"The debt ratio is a financial ratio that measures the extent
of a company’s leverage."

Dividend Yield:
"The dividend yield, expressed as a percentage,
is a financial ratio that shows how much a company pays out in
dividends each year relative to its stock price."

Net Income and Profit Margin:
"The net profit margin is equal to how much
net income or profit is generated as a percentage of revenue. Net profit margin
is the ratio of net profits to revenues for a company or business segment."

Compound Annual Growth Rate:
"Compound annual growth rate (CAGR) is
the rate of return that would be required for an investment
to grow from its beginning balance to its ending balance,
assuming the profits were reinvested at the end of
each year of the investment’s lifespan."

Profitability Index:
"The profitability index (PI)...describes an index that represents
the relationship between the costs and benefits of a proposed project."
